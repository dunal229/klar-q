# The Record

How this works. That's the whole thing:

1. **Record however's easy on your phone** — Voice Memos, Camera, whatever you
   already use. The files stay on your phone.
2. **Tell me about it here.** Talk or type — the date, what happened, how it
   felt. Even one line is enough.
3. **I write it down** into [`the-record.md`](./the-record.md) and keep it
   forever. Over time it becomes the book.

No app to learn. No uploading. Your videos and voice memos live on your phone;
this is the honest written record beside them.
